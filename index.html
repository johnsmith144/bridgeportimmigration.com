FAQ's

Q. What types of visas do you help with?

Ans.

We assist with a wide range of visas including student visas, visitor visas (including convocation), business visas, permanent residency, work permits, and family-based immigration. We also handle asylum applications, citizenship processes, and removal defense cases.

Q. How do I get started with Bridgeport Immigration?

Ans.

Getting started is easy! You can schedule a consultation through our Contact Us page or call us directly. Once we understand your needs, our team will guide you through the process step-by-step and set up your secure client portal for case management.

Q. Is my information kept confidential?

Ans.

Absolutely. We use a secure CRM system to ensure all client information, documents, and communications are kept strictly confidential. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Q. Can I still apply if another agency said my case is “risky”?

Ans.

Yes. At Bridgeport Immigration, we believe every case is unique and deserves a fair, thorough review. Many clients who were previously rejected or discouraged by others have successfully achieved their goals with our help. We offer honest, personalized guidance—always.
